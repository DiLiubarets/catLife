Your cat's age in human years




According to popular legend, one human year is the equivalent of seven 'cat years', but in reality, a one-year-old cat is much more mature than a seven-year-old child.









If your cat was a human, how old would(s) he be?

A cat reaches the approximate human age of 15 during his first year, then 25 at age 2. Each year after, a cat ages approximately four cat years for every calendar year.
